How to make the best Lasagna!

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You ll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but lets go over the basics:

Ingredients


  • meat
  • Onion and Garlic

Steps

  2. Cook the noodles.
